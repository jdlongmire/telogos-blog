## JD Longmire

Senior Enterprise Architect with a focus on AI-native infrastructure and operations. I build and operate enterprise platforms where AI isn't a bolt-on — it's the operating model.

## Human-Curated, AI-Enabled

This blog practices what it discusses. The human provides direction, judgment, editorial voice, and curation. AI enables research, drafting, analysis, and execution at a scale one person couldn't achieve alone.

Every post reflects this partnership: the architectural thinking is human; the breadth of execution is AI-enabled. Neither alone produces what both together can.

## What You'll Find Here

Honest assessments of AI maturation in the enterprise. Not vendor pitches. Not hype cycles. Field notes from someone actually building and operating AI-native infrastructure.

Topics include:
- How AI changes the staffing model for IT operations
- Self-hosted vs. SaaS in the age of AI
- Data sovereignty and why it matters more now
- The gap between AI demos and AI in production
- What "autonomous agents" actually means in practice
